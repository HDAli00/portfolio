03 — What replaced it

Same workloads. A system that heals itself.

GitOps-managed, probed, spread across zones, and small enough to actually reason about.

Git as source of truthEvery manifest reviewable. Nothing touches the cluster that isn't committed.
GitOps auto-syncContinuous reconciliation — drift gets flagged, not discovered mid-incident.
Kustomize overlaysOne base, thin per-env overlays. No forked YAML across dev/stg/prod.
EKS, probed, multi-AZReadiness + liveness probes on every deployment, spread across 3 zones.
KarpenterSpot-first autoscaling — headroom appears in seconds, not a ticket.
